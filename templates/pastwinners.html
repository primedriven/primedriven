UPCOMING WINNERS

Read below how, when & where we contact you when you win a Prime Driven Giveaway.

HOW DO I KNOW I WON?

1. winners will be picked live on our channel
2. If you are part of the winners we will reach out to you.
3. We Email the Next Steps & Paperwork.

All of Prime Driven's Giveaways are backed by American Sweepstakes.

HOW IS THE WINNER CHOSEN?

At the end of each entry window, all entries are sent to a 3rd-party Federally Compliant Sweepstakes Company who conducts the randomized computer drawing. Once a Winner is chosen, they send us the Winner's information so that we can call and congratulate them first.

Once we've contacted the Winner American Sweepstakes will reach out with the paperwork so we can confirm and announce the Winner. All our Sweepstakes are backed by American Sweepstakes.

WHEN WILL YOU CALL US?

On the approximate dates above:
1. WE TEXT a photo of your Entry.
(
2. WE EMAIL the Next Steps & Sweepstakes Paperwork.
3. WE ANNOUNCE you as the Winner on our Website and channel.
WHERE DO YOU CONTACT US?


1. WE send a Congratulatory email.
2. A SUPPORT TEAM REACH OUT TO YOU WITH THE NEXT STEP AND PAPERWORK .
4. WE ANNOUNCE you as the Winner on our Website and Socials.

*We NEVER contact the Winner VIA Social Media and we NEVER ask you to pay to receive the car.*

WHAT'S THE WINNER PROCESS?

1. We contact around the posted date.

2. You are sent the paperwork to sign.

3. We announce you as the Winner.

4. You record your Winner Interview.

5. We contact you to schedule shipping.

6. Your car is delivered FREE to you!

Timeline: The full process typically takes 2 days to 2 weeks, depending on paperwork completion, your location, time of year, and shipping availability.
