HOW IS THE WINNER CHOSEN?

At the end of each Giveaway, all entries are sent to a 3rd-party Federally Compliant Sweepstakes Company who conducts the randomized computer drawing.

Once we've contacted the Winner American Sweepstakes will reach out with the paperwork so we can confirm the Winner. All our Sweepstakes are backed by American Sweepstakes.

WHEN WILL YOU REACH OUT TO US?

Approximately 1 Day after the Giveaway Ends. The dates above are when we expect to have the Winner's name, Contact and Announce them.

There is no set time we reach out. The draw, call, confirmation and post can take 30 minutes, a few hours, a day or a few days. Please be patient!

*Any comments, messages or texts asking: "Who Won? Did you call yet? Did I Win?"⁠ or any variation will be ignored. We post the Winners as soon as possible.

HOW DO I KNOW IF I WON?

1. winners will be Announce live on our channel
2. If you are part of the winners we will reach out to you.
3. We Email the Next Steps & Paperwork.
All of Prime Driven's Giveaways are backed by American Sweepstakes.
WHAT'S THE WINNER PROCESS?

1. We contact around the posted date.

2. You are sent the paperwork to sign.

3. We announce you as the Winner.

4. You record your Winner Interview.

5. We contact you to schedule shipping.

6. Your car is delivered to you!

Timeline: The full process typically takes 2 days to 2 weeks, depending on paperwork completion, your location, time of year, and shipping availability.
